Call of Duty Mobile

Call of Duty: Mobile is a 2019 first-person shooter video game developed by TiMi Studio Group and published by Activision for Android and iOS. It was one of the largest mobile game launches in history, generating over US$480 million with 270 million downloads within a year. Experience the thrill of the iconic Call of Duty franchise, now on your mobile device.
